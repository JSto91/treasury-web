TL:DR

  • In this vote you are asked to decide what should happen to a pool of Tokens that has not been claimed for a long time
  • This Tokens are currently not migrated and the migration prohibited by the node operators
  • You have to choose from 2 options
  • The system has been build by community developers
  • To participate in the vote you need to have IOTA Tokens in the Firefly Wallet
  • 1 IOTA Tokens equals 1 Vote
  • You can initiate this vote here on the Website
  • This website can connect with your Profile in the Firefly Wallet
  • After you have connected your Firefly Wallet you can decide how much Votes you want to use for an Option
  • Confirmation of your vote happens in the Firefly Wallet, so you will be directed and Firefly Wallet opens, you need to confirm the vote using your Stronghold password, or Ledger device
  • A vote is a transaction that you send to your own address.
  • Included in this transaction is the voting data
  • You are able to change your vote untill xx.xx.21 xx:xx UTC without any consequences
  • After this time your vote will start to gain voting weight for 7 days
  • Only if you keep your vote for the full 7 days you gain the full voting weight of your tokens.
  • Starting a vote after xx.xx.21 xx:xx UTC is possible, but you can only gain a part of your full voting power and not reach 100% voting weight
  • The vote cost you nothing, no transaction fees or any other costs are involved
  • Your IOTA Tokens are aways available for you. If you move them away from the voting address your vote will not gain anymore weight
  • Votes will be counted by the community in their Hornet nodes.
  • The Hornet nodes will produce the count and the community has always access to this voting Data that is dispalyed here on the website
